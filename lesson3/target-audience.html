Target Audience

Better Weather Idaho will be used by citizens living in Southern Idaho. They will be people who work or have hobbies outside, or someone who likes to make sure they are dressed correctly for the weather. These people like to the prepared and plan ahead and rely on an accurate weather website that can be easily accessed by all devices.

Scenarios

  • What is the weather like right now? What about later today?
  • How can I prepare so that I can work or enjoy the day without the weather affecting me too much?
  • What should I wear today so I am comfortable?
  • How will the weather affect my day and week?
  • What is the weather like in neighboring towns?
