Hick's Law

Wendy's
www.wendys.com

I felt like Wendy's front page showed Hick's law well because there aren't very many buttons to click once the page is first loaded. Instead of having a bunch of specials being shown on smaller images, or 4 or 5 specials scrolling by, they just have one big one.

White Space

Apple
www.apple.com

I have always been impressed with how sleek Apple's pages are. They use white space really well so that the focus is on their technology. Their design is very clean, with just the right amount of information to pique interest. I can see why so many people like their products.

Alignment

ABC
abc.go.com/shows

ABC's show page stuck out to me. I have never realized it, but having the TV show's pictures alined makes it easy to scroll through and find exactly what the user is looking for. I also like that the titles are centered. I think it looks better than it would be if they were centered.
